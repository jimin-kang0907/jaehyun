Who is Jaehyun?

Jeong Yun-o, professionally known as Jaehyun, is a South Korean singer, rapper, television host and actor. He is a member of South Korean boy group NCT and its subunits NCT U, NCT 127, and NCT DoJaeJung. He is born on 14th, Feburary, 1997, thus has a title of Valentine's Boy. He is currently considered as one of the most famous influencer of Korea.
